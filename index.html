About Us

Anito's secret garden restaurant where people from East to West come to meet, eat and drink together. Great food, cool drinks and unbelievable ambiance with a cool flowing creek right under your table. Our garden surrounds you with Acasia and Mango trees, variety of orchids and many other tropical plants and flowers.

Accommodations

A Polynesian House with 4 suites that can accommodate up to 30 adults in a beautiful garden setting with a pure, fresh mountain stream just outside your door. Your group or family will reside together in complete comfort. The air-conditioned units are furnished with tiled floors and feature a bathroom, a shower, free WiFi in lobby, patio and views over the garden.
